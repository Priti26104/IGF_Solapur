About Us

What is IGF Solapur?

ISKCON Girls Forum (IGF) Solapur is a devotional community under the guidance of H.H. Lokanath Swami Maharaj, lovingly led by H.G. Vrajavadhu Devi Dasi. We are a group of girls committed to practicing and sharing Krishna consciousness through sadhana, study, and seva.

📿
Daily Sadhana TrackingChanting 16 rounds, attending Mangal Arati, daily reading and hearing — tracked through our IGF Tracker app.
📖
Bhagavad Gita StudyOnline courses, Japa sessions, quizzes, and group readings of Srila Prabhupada's books.
🌺
Festivals & SevaFrom Janmashtami kirtan to Rangoli Seva — girls actively participate in all temple services.
